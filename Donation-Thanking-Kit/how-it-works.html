Works with whatever email you already use

Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo — it doesn't matter. The tool fills in the message for you and hands it to your normal email. It has no mail account of its own, so there's no password to give it.

  • It tidies the list Fixes CAPS, reads “twelve hundred” as $1,200, merges the same donor entered twice.
  • It matches the words to the gift A $15 first-timer and a $1,200 regular do not get the same sentence.
  • It holds back anyone incomplete Missing a name, amount, or email? Flagged for you, never sent.

To use it yourself

  • 1. Install Thonny Free, from thonny.org. It's the only thing you install.
  • 2. Press Play It makes you a blank donor sheet. Type your donors in and save it.
  • 3. Press Play again Out come the letters and the emails, ready for you to send.
